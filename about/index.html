About me

Howdy. My name is Spencer. I am a skateboard and concert photographer from Joliet, Illinois.

I started taking photos in the spring of 2021. I quickly found out that I enjoyed skateboard photography, and I began spending the fall of that year traveling to skateparks around Illinois and Wisconsin to capture some very talented skaters.

In 2022, I started attending basement shows and taking photos whenever I had the chance. I immediately fell in love with concert photography. I absolutely love capturing the magic of live performances. There is something so special about capturing a moment of a performance in a way that gives the viewer a glimpse into what it was like to be there. I knew that I wanted the opportunity to take photos of my favorite bands, so I created this portfolio to share what I am capable of.

Since then, I have had some wonderful opporunities to capture the performances of bands like Deerhoof, Messer Chups, and umru. I have so much fun doing this, and I enjoy sharing it with you!

Thank you for checking out my portfolio. If you would like to get in contact with me, check out the contact page on the side!
